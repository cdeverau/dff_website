We aim to identify and support organizations that make the world a better place.

Our Story

The Deveraux Family Foundation was established with a simple but enduring belief: that private resources, directed with care and intention, can amplify the work of organizations already transforming lives. What began as a family conversation around shared values has grown into a focused vehicle for meaningful giving.

We approach philanthropy as a long-term partnership — not a transaction. We seek out organizations whose leadership is visionary, whose impact is measurable, and whose communities would be genuinely diminished without them.

Our Mission

Our mission is to identify, support, and sustain non-profit and not-for-profit organizations working toward a more equitable, sustainable, and compassionate world. We prioritize relationships built on trust, transparency, and a genuine commitment to the communities our partners serve.
